About

I started my journey as a mechanic, with a passion for electronics since childhood. In 10th grade, I discovered my love for programming and started attending various training centers to enhance my skills. By the time I reached 12th grade, I participated in a provincial-level science and engineering competition and achieved an award.

After completing high school, I decided to pursue my dream of studying abroad in Japan. However, I faced a setback and couldn't fulfill that aspiration. Undeterred, I made the decision to stay in Vietnam and work for a while. I joined Smarthome Tiến Cường, a company specializing in smart home technology, where I worked as an electrician and a smart home technician. People often refer to me as a "hands-on" technician, given my practical skills. During my time there, I gained valuable experience and learned from various professional challenges.

Unfortunately, in October 2021, I encountered an accident that halted all my plans, including my dream of studying in Japan. From that, I have decided to pursue graphic design and enhance my knowledge in the field of embedded programming. Currently, I am studying at the Ho Chi Minh City College of Information Technology to broaden my knowledge and prepare for my future.

My journey so far has been a combination of passion, setbacks, and resilience. I believe that every experience, whether positive or negative, contributes to personal growth. I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead and remain committed to my goal of achieving success in the field of information technology.
